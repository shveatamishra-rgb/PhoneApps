# Devotional Content Standard

Every image must pass a human review before App Store release or use in a paid
collection.

## Review each deity

- Face, eyes, hands, feet, and body anatomy are coherent.
- Arm count and hand positions are appropriate.
- Sacred objects are recognizable and held correctly.
- Tilak, ornaments, clothing, vehicle, and companion figures are respectful.
- No accidental text, logos, signatures, or malformed symbols appear.
- No unrelated deity attributes have been merged into the subject.
- The tone is devotional, not frightening, sexualized, comic, or promotional.

## Product copy

- Use `HD` or `sacred wallpaper`; do not describe the current 941x1672 assets
  as 8K.
- Do not promise wealth, healing, protection, or outcomes as guaranteed facts.
- Present mantra meanings as simple devotional explanations, not authoritative
  translations for every lineage.
- Add corrections promptly when a devotee identifies an iconographic issue.

## Future production

Generate future wallpaper masters at a taller iPhone-safe ratio with generous
space around crowns, halos, hands, and feet. Keep a clean source master, a
social 9:16 export, and an app wallpaper export as separate deliverables.
