# Divine Stillness Om Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Divine Stillness Om is designed to work without an account. The app stores
favorites, preferred mantra, daily japa count, daily darshan streak, and
reminder preferences locally on the user's device.

## Information collection

The app does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. It does
not include advertising trackers or third-party analytics. Because all of this
data stays on the device, deleting the app deletes the data.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. The developer receives purchase
entitlement information needed to unlock Pro features but does not receive the
user's full payment-card details.

## Notifications

If the user enables a daily darshan reminder, notification permission is used
only to schedule that local reminder. The reminder can be disabled in the app
or in iPhone Settings.

## Photos

Photo-library add permission is requested only when the user chooses to save a
devotional wallpaper. The app does not read the user's photo library.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy will be updated before adding analytics, accounts, cloud sync,
advertising, or any other data collection.
